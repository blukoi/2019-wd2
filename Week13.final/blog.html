In keeping with the summer blockbuster tradition, as we head into July we'll be featuring a few more of the big-budget crowd pleasers in our collection. The illustrious Malcolm McDowell stopped by Grauman's Egyptian to grace us with a lengthy Q&A following a double feature of Cat People and Time After Time. Topics touched on Stanley Kubrick, Giorgrio Moroder, and more. Click through for the video as well as a full transcription.
