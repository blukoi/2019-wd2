About Us

At American Cinematheque we have the honor and the privilege of protecting and restoring some of our most important cinematic treasures. Our goal is to save our film history so that the public can enjoy it for years to come.

We're proud to count among our devoted members the likes of Sydney Pollack, Martin Scorsese, Mike Nichols, Francis Coppola, William Friedkin, Melvin Van Peebles, Brian Grazer, Joe Dante, Paula Wagner, Steve Tisch, and many more.
