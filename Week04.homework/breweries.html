Los Angeles Breweries

Here you'll find a listing of breweries in LA, plus a few other must-visit hot spots.

(Alphabetical order)

Blackbeard's Crafts

Founded in 2016, Blackbeard's Crafts is a small wine and beer shop catering to a busy neighborhood in West LA. They love newcomers to the beer world and will help you explore all of the interesting new beers available, but also do their best to get the rare and limited kinda beer that the Untappd addicts love.

Website | Instagram

Bottle Logic

One of the premiere breweries in LA, Bottle Logic is actually in Anaheim. They tout some of the highest rated, most sought after beers in the area. While their online bottle sales usually sell out within the hour, making their to-go selection basically non-existant, they keep most of their unbelievable brews on-top.
