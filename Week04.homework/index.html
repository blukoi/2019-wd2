Thanks to your love and support, we're turning SEVEN this year. We invite you to come celebrate with us with good beers and good times while giving back to our community. The 11th annual LA Beer Festival follows last year's sold out event, and this year's promises to be even better. The event will feature dozens of international and domestic beers, over a dozen food trucks (food sold separately) as well as live bands, Power 106 DJ, and a dueling piano bar.
